About the Friends Group

Friends of Russia Dock Woodlands is a community-led group supporting the protection, enhancement, and long-term care of Russia Dock Woodlands and Stave Hill Ecological Park.

Who We Are

Inspired by the structure of the current Friends website, this page presents the organisation as a local residents’ group working to protect the woodland, improve the site, and respond to issues such as signage, maintenance, and ecological pressures. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Our Mission

To protect, enhance, and promote Russia Dock Woodlands for the enjoyment of all, while supporting biodiversity, education, and community wellbeing.

What We Do

We help improve the woodland through community action, ecological stewardship, public awareness, and dialogue with local stakeholders.

Why It Matters

The woodland is an important urban ecological asset that contributes to habitat quality, recreation, climate resilience, and local identity.

Governance and Community Care

This upgraded website prototype expands the traditional Friends-group model by creating a clearer link between community participation, ecological communication, and local governance.
