About This Platform

This website prototype reimagines the Friends of Russia Dock Woodlands website as a participatory platform. Rather than only sharing information, it helps connect visitors, volunteers, and organisers through accessible ecological information, community feedback, and opportunities for action.

Why This Matters

Urban woodlands play an important role in biodiversity protection, flood resilience, environmental education, and public wellbeing. By improving digital access and community participation, this platform supports a more inclusive and transparent approach to environmental governance.
