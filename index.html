A Community Woodland at the Heart of Rotherhithe

Russia Dock Woodlands and Stave Hill Ecological Park are valuable urban green spaces that support biodiversity, public wellbeing, and local community life. This platform connects ecological knowledge, volunteering opportunities, and community participation.

About the Woodland

Friends of Russia Dock Woodlands is a community-led group supporting the protection and improvement of the woodland and surrounding ecological park. The site is not only an environmental asset, but also a space for education, recreation, and collective care.
