A Community Woodland at the Heart of Rotherhithe

Russia Dock Woodlands and Stave Hill Ecological Park are valuable urban green spaces that support biodiversity, public wellbeing, and local community life. This platform brings together ecological knowledge, ecosystem value, hydrological understanding, shared governance, volunteering opportunities, and community participation.

About this platform

This website prototype reimagines the Friends of Russia Dock Woodlands site as more than an information page. It is designed as a participatory platform connecting ecology, environmental understanding, community engagement, and more inclusive governance.

Community Voice

We extend the traditional community website model by adding a digital participation space. Visitors and residents can share feedback, complete surveys, and contribute ideas for the future of the woodland. In this way, the platform supports not only information access, but also public participation in shaping environmental management.

Share Feedback

Why this matters

Russia Dock Woodlands is not only a place to visit, but also a valuable urban ecological system. Through clearer information, public-facing environmental knowledge, and opportunities for participation, this platform supports a more inclusive and resilient approach to woodland stewardship.
